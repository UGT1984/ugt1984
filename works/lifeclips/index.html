LifeCLIPS

Client
iDEAKITT
Category
Web Service
Role
UI/UX Design, Front-end
Writing can be inspired by many different occasions. Sometimes for sharing with close friends or sometimes for self-reflection and therapy.
LifeCLIPS offers a text-only diary platform so that such people can write without hesitation.

The design priority of LifeCLIPS was to create simple, clean and sophisticated visuals as much as possible
so users can focus on writing their diary / blog in a space where the design of typography beauty is highlighted.
This project design aligned with my personal and professional design beliefs: Clean and simple, with minimal decoration.
